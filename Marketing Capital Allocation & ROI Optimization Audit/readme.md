PROJECT CASE STUDY: Marketing Capital Allocation & ROI Optimization Audit
Analyst: Saad
Dataset: 11 Campaigns | $30.8M Total Marketing Expenditure
Objective: Identifying structural inefficiencies to improve portfolio-wide ROI.
1. THE OVERVIEW: Performance Benchmarking
The current marketing portfolio operates at an average ROI of 0.37. While the aggregate return is positive, a granular audit reveals significant variance between channels. High-performing segments are currently offsetting substantial capital losses in others, masking a critical need for budget reallocation.
2. CORE METHODOLOGY

    Data Integrity: Standardized a multi-channel dataset using Python to ensure 100% calculation accuracy across 11 key performance variables.
    Performance Lenses: Engineered custom KPIs—including Cost Per Order (CPO) and Return on Investment (ROI)—to evaluate campaign efficiency beyond simple top-funnel metrics (Clicks/Impressions).
    Segment Analysis: Categorized spend into Influencer, Media, Search, and Social to identify "Order Share" vs. "Capital Consumption."

3. KEY FINDINGS: Volume vs. Efficiency
A. The Influencer Channel (High-Yield Growth)

    The Data: This category maintains the highest efficiency in the portfolio with a 1.54 ROI.
    Key Driver: youtube_blogger successfully scaled to a $4.05M spend while delivering a 2.77 ROI.
    Strategic Value: This channel provides 37.5% of total orders, proving it is the most scalable asset for future growth.

B. The Social Category (Capital Inefficiency)

    The Data: Despite representing the largest budget allocation ($13.7M), the Social category is operating at a -0.14 ROI.
    Primary Friction Point: facebook_lal shows a critical performance gap with an $8,986 CPO and a -0.88 ROI.
    The "Wide" Strategy Leak: Broad-targeting campaigns (google_wide, facebook_tier2) are consistently underperforming compared to intent-based "Hot" targeting.

4. STRATEGIC REALLOCATION PLAN (Projected Impact)  
PRIORITY	RECOMMENDATION	RATIONALE	CAPITAL AT RISK  
🚀 SCALE	YouTube Blogger	Proven ability to maintain high ROI (2.77) at scale.	Move $2M+ here  
🛑 CEASE	Facebook LAL	Unsustainable CPO ($8,986) and negative net return.	$2.6M Inefficient Spend  
📉 REDUCE	Broad Targeting	google_wide and facebook_tier2 show negative margins.	$6.9M Inefficient Spend  
📈 TEST	Facebook Retargeting	High efficiency (1.01 ROI) on low volume; potential for scaling.	Increase allocation  
5. BUSINESS CONCLUSION & NEXT STEPS  
By addressing the $9.6M in underperforming spend identified in this audit, the business has a clear path to shift from a "break-even" 0.37 ROI toward a projected 1.10+ ROI.
